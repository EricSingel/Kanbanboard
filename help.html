How does our app work?


1. Go to the Add Task section and fill in the empty fields. Then click on the "Create Task" button to create a new task.


2. Now go to the "Backlog" section. There you will see the task (with all the details) that you created earlier. If you want, you can click on it to send it to the board.


3. Click on "Board" to see all the tasks you have created and sent to the board. In this section you can click on the individual tasks to see them in more detail or drag and drop them into the desired section ("To do", "In progress", "Testing", "Done"). Drag and drop them into the desired section.
